The Core of the problem

"No Matter what I Try, it just keeps coming back"

That is the experience you will have unless you address the root cause of the problem.

A lot of solutions to address lower back problems can often feel really good in the moment and provide a new sense of relief



Other times, they can help to reduce the pain and swelling, or dull it enough to where you can live by day to day



However, these solutions will never be lasting, they will come back while the true issue keeps progressing worse and worse so you're continually stuck going back for these temporary treatments, which will just add up and up



The reason why this happens is because these solutions such as chiropractic care, physical therapy, epidurals, stretches, and more never address the root cause of what's giving you this problem in the first place.



All they do is try to act as a bandaid to cover up the surface level symptoms, which will eventually fall off, and half the time it won't even work effectively.



In order to truly fix this problem, you need to understand the root cause of this issue that lies behind movement, which will offer a permanent solution, giving your body no reason to feel the pain in the first place.



Here, I break down in detail what the root cause is, and how to effectively address it. See for yourself.
